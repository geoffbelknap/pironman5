# Pironman 5 Pro Max Hardware Highlights

- **Chassis & Dimensions**: 140.9 × 77.0 × 138.7 mm, dark anodized aluminum body with dark acrylic side panels and spring-loaded microSD slot.
- **Power**: USB-C 5 V/5 A input (27 W official or SunFounder 27 W PD supply strongly recommended, especially when NVMe & accessories are active).
- **Cooling**: Tower heatsink with PWM fan plus 3x addressable RGB PWM chassis fans (GPIO controlled).
- **Displays & UI**:
  - 4.3" 800×480 DSI capacitive touchscreen (secondary display or dashboard).
  - 0.96" 128×64 OLED with vibration tap-to-wake; pages: mix, performance, ips, disk (extendable via software patches).
  - Safe retro-style metal power button mapped to wake/page events & graceful shutdown.
  - IR receiver (38 kHz) and external GPIO expansion header.
- **Multimedia**: Stereo 3 W speakers, 5 MP camera support.
- **Lighting**: Six onboard WS2812B LEDs plus three RGB fans (synchronized) with controllable color/brightness/effects/LED count.
- **Storage & Expansion**: Integrated PCIe Gen2 switch with dual M.2 M-key slots (2230/2242/2260/2280). Supports dual SSDs, SSD + AI accelerators (Hailo-8/8L), or dual AI cards. Configurable RAID 0/1 for NAS builds.
- **Connectivity Layout**: Rear-aligned dual HDMI, USB-C power, 2×USB 2.0, 2×USB 3.0, Gigabit Ethernet, plus labelled GPIO extender for easy prototyping.
- **Optional Modules**: RTL-SDR, NAS roles, AI “Think·Talk·Drive” workflows referenced in upstream docs.
